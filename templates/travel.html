Summer 2019 Travel

After graduation, my best friend and I backpacked in Europe for 6 weeks. We had a crazy, insane trip, and here are some pictures in the places we visited! Use the interactive map to see that places we went and a few pictures in each place.

(You must be signed into Lionmail to view)

Personally, it's not possible for me to say where my favorite places were. But I think I had some of the most unique experiences while sailing in Greece and just exploring Barcelona. Another mutual friend of ours was sailing with his dad in Greece, and invited us on the boat. It was one of the coolest things I will ever do. I slept under the stars every night, and swam in the cleanest, bluest water ever. We occasionally saw dolphins as we sailed, which was spectacular. Berlin was magical in my opinion, partially because it was the first stop on the trip, so the excitement of having just graduated and just being with my friend in a new country was incredible. I think about this trip almost every day, especially during these quarantine-times because the kind of freedom to explore we had seems so foreign to me now.
